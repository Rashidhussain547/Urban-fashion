About us

Urban Fashion Studio is the ideal place for people who are keen on maintaining style without compromising with the quality of products. The company has received immense popularity in the clothing industry for offering designer leather jackets of high quality at a-friendly price. Our craftsmen use latest technology to manufacture jackets, coats, blazers, and leather accessories at the highest possible quality. Each and every jacket manufactured is tested and reviewed for quality assurance. Every leather jacket manufactured by UFS speaks to the quality of our products and the expertise of designers. Biker jackets, Bomber jackets, blazers, or coats; whatever you purchase from UFS is guaranteed to outshine any competitors price and quality. Whatever the occasion, UFS will have the perfect jacket for you. Every jacket is tailored according to the size provided by the customers, assuring a picture perfect fit. From tailor to quality analysts, every employee of Urban Fashion Studio believes in following a simple idea: to keep every customer satisfied. To abide by this idea, we test each and every product thoroughly before delivery. We are constantly updating our website and work, and always welcome suggestions from our valuable clientele. Our customer help desk is available to offer you the any assistance for returns or exchanges of items that may not meet the expectations of the customer. All our products are made from high quality genuine leather. The leather is collected from best animal skins that are available on the market. We are manufacturer of leather goods, so for that reason we can provide leather jackets with high quality at fraction of a cost as compared to the same quality leather jackets that are being sold by other brands.
